## Where Calldrip Wins

- **Price:** At $99/mo per user, Calldrip is significantly cheaper than any LeadCatch plan. For contractors who just want faster callbacks and are available to answer, it's cost-effective.
- **Voice connection:** Some leads prefer talking to a real person immediately. Calldrip facilitates that by bridging a live call.
- **Simplicity:** Calldrip does one thing (speed-to-call) and does it well. Less complexity.
- **Multi-industry:** Calldrip works for any business, not just roofing.

---

## Where LeadCatch Wins

- **The fatal flaw of callback tools:** Calldrip only works if the contractor answers the phone. But the whole reason the lead went to voicemail is because the contractor didn't answer. Calldrip calls the contractor back — but if the contractor is on a roof, in a meeting, or driving, they miss Calldrip's call too. Then the lead gets nothing. LeadCatch doesn't need the contractor at all.
- **24/7 coverage:** A lead who calls at 8 PM on a Saturday gets an instant text from Alex. With Calldrip, they get nothing until the contractor is available.
- **Full conversation handling:** Calldrip connects a call. The contractor still has to qualify the lead, answer questions, and schedule the appointment. Alex does all of that automatically.
- **Follow-up persistence:** If a lead doesn't respond to the first text, Alex follows up 6 more times over 14 days. Calldrip's follow-up is minimal.
- **Zero effort:** The contractor using Calldrip still has to answer calls, qualify leads, and schedule. The contractor using LeadCatch checks their calendar and shows up to the estimate.

## The Core Question

**Do you want to talk to leads faster, or do you want leads talked to for you?**

If you're a contractor who's available to answer your phone most of the time and you just need leads routed faster — Calldrip at $99/mo is a solid deal.

If you're a contractor who misses 60%+ of calls because you're actually doing roofing work, and you need a system that handles the entire lead conversation without you — LeadCatch is the answer.

Most roofing contractors are in the second category. That's why they're losing $47,000 a year to voicemail. Calling them back faster doesn't help if they can't answer that call either.

## Who Should Choose What

**Choose Calldrip if:**
- You're available to answer your phone 90%+ of the time during business hours
- You prefer live phone conversations over text-based qualification
- Budget is your primary constraint ($99/mo vs. $597+/mo)
- You have office staff who can handle overflow calls

**Choose LeadCatch if:**
- You miss calls regularly because you're on job sites
- You need after-hours and weekend coverage
- You want leads qualified and appointments booked without your involvement
- You're willing to pay more for a system that generates measurable ROI
