## Where Podium Wins

- **All-in-one platform:** Podium includes webchat, payments, and review management in one tool. If you want a Swiss army knife, Podium covers more ground.
- **Review management:** Podium's review features are mature and well-integrated. LeadCatch has automated review requests, but Podium offers more review analytics and response tools.
- **Lower starting price:** Podium starts around $399/mo, which is less than LeadCatch's Starter plan.
- **Payment processing:** Podium lets you collect payments via text. LeadCatch doesn't handle payments.

---

## Where LeadCatch Wins

- **Fully automated conversations:** Podium sends you a notification when a lead messages. You (or your staff) still have to respond. LeadCatch handles the entire conversation automatically — qualification, objection handling, and booking — with zero human input.
- **Speed guarantee:** LeadCatch responds in under 60 seconds, every time, including nights and weekends. Podium's response time depends on how fast your team checks the inbox.
- **No staff required:** The #1 reason contractors buy Podium and fail with it: they don't have someone dedicated to monitoring the inbox. LeadCatch eliminates this problem entirely.
- **Roofing expertise:** Alex is trained on roofing-specific conversations. Podium is industry-agnostic — which means you're starting from scratch on templates and workflows.
- **Storm Surge:** Weather-triggered proactive outreach. Podium has nothing comparable.

## The Real Problem with Podium for Roofers

Podium is a great product. But it's a tool, not a system. It requires someone — you, your office manager, your sales rep — to:

1. Monitor the inbox
2. Respond to every message
3. Qualify the lead
4. Schedule the appointment
5. Follow up when they go quiet

That's the exact problem LeadCatch solves. If you had someone to do all that, you wouldn't need either product.

The contractors who get the most from Podium are the ones with dedicated office staff. The contractors who get the most from LeadCatch are the ones who don't.

**Choose Podium if:**

**Choose LeadCatch if:**
- You're an owner-operator who can't monitor an inbox all day
- You want conversations handled automatically, not just routed to you
