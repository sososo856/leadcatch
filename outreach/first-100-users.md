## Channel 1: Google LSA Prospecting (Direct Outbound)

**Why:** Every roofing contractor running Google Local Services Ads is actively paying for leads and has the budget for LeadCatch. They're the perfect ICP.

**Daily actions:**
- Search "roofing contractor [city]" in 5 storm belt cities per day
- Identify 10 contractors running LSA (look for "Google Guaranteed" badge)
- Call each one and intentionally let it ring to voicemail
- Note which ones don't call back within 2 hours
- Send those contractors a cold email or LinkedIn DM with the data: "I called your business at [time]. It's been [X] hours and nobody's called me back. Here's what that's costing you."

**Daily target:** 10 contractors contacted, 2 conversations started
**Expected conversion:** 5-8% of conversations become clients
**Tools:** Google search, Twilio (for test calls), LinkedIn, Brevo (for email)

## Channel 2: Facebook Groups (Community Infiltration)

**Why:** Roofing contractor Facebook groups have 5,000-50,000 members. Owners are active, asking for advice, complaining about leads. Perfect for warm outreach.

**Groups to join:**
- Roofing Contractors Network (47K members)
- Storm Chasers — Roofing Sales (32K members)
- Roofing Business Owner (21K members)
- Contractor Growth Network (18K members)
- Roofing Sales Mastermind (12K members)

**Daily actions:**
- Comment on 5 posts per day with genuine, helpful advice (no pitching)
- Share 1 value post per week (missed call stats, speed-to-lead data, ROI calculator)
- DM any contractor who posts about lead problems, missed calls, or slow follow-up
- DM format: "Hey, saw your post about [problem]. We built something that might help — want me to send you a 2-minute video?"

**Daily target:** 5 comments, 3 DMs sent, 1 demo booked per week
**Expected conversion:** 10-15% of DMs lead to a call; 20-30% of calls close

## Channel 3: Referral Programme (Client-Driven Growth)

**Why:** A happy contractor telling another contractor "this thing books me 3 extra jobs a week" is the highest-converting sales channel that exists.

**Structure:**
- $500 cash or 1 free month per referred client who signs and pays
- Referred client gets $300 off first month
- Referral ask triggered at Day 14 after first positive outcome

**Daily actions:**
- Send NPS survey to every client at Day 7
- Any client who scores 8+ gets a personal referral ask within 24 hours
- Monthly "Referral Leaderboard" email to all clients
- Quarterly: top referrer gets a bonus ($1,000 or free month)

**Daily target:** 1 referral ask per active client per month
**Expected conversion:** 20% of happy clients refer; 40% of referred leads close
**Why this scales:** At 20 clients, 4 refer per month, 1.6 close = 1-2 organic new clients/month

## Channel 4: LinkedIn Outbound (Targeted Owner Outreach)

**Why:** Roofing company owners are on LinkedIn. They have "Owner" or "President" in their title. Easy to find, filter, and message.

**Search filters:**
- Title: "Owner" OR "President" OR "CEO"
- Industry: Construction
- Company headcount: 2-50
- Keywords: "roofing" OR "roof" OR "contractor"
- Geography: TX, FL, CO, OH, GA, IL, OK, TN, NC

**Daily actions:**
- Send 20 connection requests per day with a note: "Hey {name} — I help roofing contractors book more jobs from the calls they're already missing. Would love to connect."
- Follow up with every accepted connection within 24 hours with the Channel 1 pitch
- Post 2 LinkedIn posts per week (build-in-public content, industry stats, client wins)

**Daily target:** 20 connection requests, 5 follow-up DMs, 1 call booked
**Expected conversion:** 15% accept, 10% of accepts respond to DM, 20% of responses book a call, 30% of calls close
**Math:** 20 requests/day x 15% x 10% x 20% x 30% = ~0.18 clients/day = ~5 clients/month at scale

## Channel 5: Content + SEO (Inbound Engine)

**Why:** Contractors Google their problems. "How to follow up with roofing leads" and "best missed call service for contractors" are real searches with buyer intent. This channel compounds.

**Content plan (first 30 days):**
- Landing page (done): leadcatch.homes
- Blog posts (1 per week):
  - "How Roofing Contractors Lose $47,000/Year to Voicemail"
  - "Speed to Lead: Why 60 Seconds Makes or Breaks a Roofing Job"
  - "LeadCatch vs. Hatch vs. Podium: Which AI Follow-Up Tool Is Best for Roofers?"
  - "The Real Cost of Missed Calls for Roofing Contractors (Calculator)"
- YouTube video: "I Called 50 Roofing Companies. Here's How Many Called Back."
- Lead magnet: Missed Call Revenue Calculator (captures email, delivers instant value)

**Daily actions:**
- Write or outline 1 blog post per week (batch on Sundays)
- Share every post on X, LinkedIn, and in Facebook groups
- Reply to every "roofing leads" or "contractor CRM" tweet with value (not a pitch)
- Optimize landing page based on Hotjar/analytics weekly

**Daily target:** 1 piece of content shared, 5 social interactions
**Expected conversion:** 2-3% of blog visitors sign up for lead magnet; 5-10% of lead magnet signups become clients over 60 days
