# LeadCatch AI — Demo Video Script
# Purpose: 90-second product demo video script
# Date: 2026-04-16

---

## Duration: 90 seconds
## Format: Screen recording + voiceover (Dan on camera for hook and CTA)

---

**[0:00–0:03] HOOK (Dan on camera)**

"What happens when a homeowner calls your roofing company and nobody picks up?"

**[0:03–0:08] THE PROBLEM (Screen: phone ringing, going to voicemail)**

"Nothing. They get voicemail. They hang up. They call the next guy on Google. You just lost a $12,000 job and you'll never know it happened."

**[0:08–0:18] THE STAT (Screen: animated stat cards)**

"62% of service calls to contractors go unanswered. 78% of callers hire the first company that responds. The average contractor loses $47,000 a year to missed calls."

**[0:18–0:25] THE SOLUTION (Screen: LeadCatch dashboard)**

"LeadCatch fixes this. When a call goes unanswered, our AI assistant Alex texts the caller back in under 60 seconds."

**[0:25–0:50] THE WALKTHROUGH (Screen: live SMS conversation between Alex and a lead)**

"Watch what happens. A homeowner calls. No answer. Within 60 seconds, Alex sends: 'Hey Sarah, this is Alex from ABC Roofing — saw you just called. What did you need help with today?'

Sarah replies: 'I think I have hail damage.'

Alex asks about insurance, location, and urgency — then books the estimate right on your Google Calendar.

Three minutes. Zero effort. One booked job."

**[0:50–1:05] THE FOLLOW-UP (Screen: follow-up sequence visualization)**

"And if the lead doesn't respond right away? Alex follows up 7 times over 14 days. Most responses come from follow-up 2 through 4 — the messages your sales rep never sends."

**[1:05–1:20] THE RESULTS (Screen: dashboard metrics — appointments booked, response time, revenue recovered)**

"Our clients book 2 to 4 extra jobs per week. At your average job value, that's $30,000 to $100,000 per month in pipeline that was going to voicemail."

**[1:20–1:30] THE CTA (Dan on camera)**

"There's nothing for you to install. Nothing to learn. One onboarding call, and Alex goes to work for you 24/7. If it doesn't pay for itself in 30 days, you get a full refund. Book your demo at leadcatch.homes."

---

## Production Notes

- **Music:** Low, clean electronic — not corporate, not hype. Think Stripe or Linear product videos.
- **Pace:** Fast but not rushed. Every sentence carries weight.
- **Screen recordings:** Use real Alex conversations (anonymized). Show the actual SMS interface, not mockups.
- **Text overlays:** Key stats appear as bold text overlays during the stat section.
- **End card:** LeadCatch logo + "leadcatch.homes" + "Book a Demo" button
- **Thumbnail:** Dan on camera with text overlay: "This AI Books Roofing Jobs in 60 Seconds"
