## What the Buyer Gets

### 1. Personalized Lead Response Audit (PDF, delivered within 24 hours)
Dan personally calls the buyer's roofing business 3 times at different hours (morning, afternoon, evening) and documents:
- How long before voicemail picks up
- Whether anyone calls back, and how long it takes
- What the voicemail message says (and what's wrong with it)
- How the experience compares to their top 3 local competitors (we call them too)
- A scored rubric: response speed, voicemail quality, callback speed, competitor comparison
- Specific recommendations for improvement

**Deliverable:** 2-page PDF with a personalized "Lead Response Score" out of 100 and a prioritized fix list.

### 2. 5 Proven SMS Templates for Roofing Contractors
Pre-written text message templates that any contractor can copy-paste and use immediately:

**Template 1: Missed Call Response**
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. Saw you just called — sorry I missed it. What's going on with your roof?"

**Template 2: Form Submission Follow-Up**
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. Got your request for an estimate. Quick question — is this storm damage or something else? Want to make sure we send the right crew."

**Template 3: No-Show Re-Engagement**
"Hey [Name], I had you down for an estimate today at [time] but it looks like we missed each other. Want to reschedule? I have [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] open."

**Template 4: Post-Estimate Follow-Up**
"Hey [Name], just wanted to follow up on the estimate we did last [day]. Any questions I can answer? Happy to walk through the numbers again if it helps."

**Template 5: Review Request**
"Hey [Name], glad we could take care of the roof for you. If you have a minute, a quick Google review would mean a lot. Here's the link: [URL]. Thanks!"

### 3. Bonus: Speed-to-Lead Cheat Sheet
One-page guide on why response time matters, what the data says, and how to set up a basic auto-text system using free tools (Google Voice + Zapier). This is the "DIY" version of what LeadCatch automates.

The tripwire creates three conversion moments:

1. **The audit reveals the problem.** When a contractor sees that their Lead Response Score is 23/100 and their competitor is at 67/100, the pain becomes tangible. The audit ends with: "Want to fix this permanently? LeadCatch handles all of this automatically for $2,997/mo. Here's a link to book a demo."

2. **The templates show effort.** The contractor tries the SMS templates and realizes: "This works, but I still have to send these manually. I still miss calls. I still forget to follow up." The upgrade pitch: "What if this happened automatically, 24/7, without you touching your phone?"

3. **The cheat sheet plants the seed.** The DIY speed-to-lead setup shows them it's possible — but also how much ongoing effort it requires. LeadCatch becomes the "done-for-you" version of what they just learned.
