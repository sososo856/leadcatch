Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is LeadCatch? ▼
LeadCatch is a done-for-you AI lead conversion system for roofing contractors. When a lead calls and gets no answer, our AI assistant Alex texts them back within 60 seconds, qualifies them through natural conversation, and books an estimate on your calendar. It works 24/7 with zero effort from you.
How is this different from Podium or Hatch? ▼
Podium and Hatch are tools you manage. LeadCatch is a system that runs itself. With Podium, someone on your team still has to respond. With Hatch, you still have to build campaigns. With LeadCatch, you connect your lead sources in one call and Alex handles everything — qualification, objection handling, scheduling — automatically.
Do I need to know anything about technology? ▼
No. That's the point. We handle every technical aspect in one 60-minute onboarding call. You give us access to your lead sources and calendar, and we configure everything. After that, you just watch your calendar fill up.
What results can I expect? ▼
Most clients see a 25-40% improvement in lead-to-appointment rate within 30 days. That translates to 2-4 extra booked jobs per week. At an average job value of $12,000, that's $24,000-$48,000 in additional monthly pipeline from the same leads you're already getting.
What if it doesn't work? ▼
If LeadCatch doesn't generate at least $2,997 in demonstrable recovered revenue in your first 30 days, we refund your first month in full. No questions asked. We track everything — conversations, appointments, pipeline — so the results are measurable, not theoretical.
Can leads tell they're talking to AI? ▼
No. Alex communicates in short, natural text messages — the same way a real team member would. No robotic language, no "How can I assist you today?" scripts. Alex uses the lead's name, acknowledges what they said, and moves toward the next step. Leads consistently think Alex is a real person.
What lead sources does it connect to? ▼
LeadCatch connects to Google Local Services Ads (LSA), website contact forms, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Yelp, Google Ads leads, and any other source that supports webhooks or form submissions. If a lead comes in somewhere, we can connect it.
How long does setup take? ▼
One 60-minute onboarding call. We connect your lead sources, sync your calendar, customize Alex for your brand, and run test conversations. Most clients are fully live within 24 hours of the call.
