## Day 1 (Monday): The Announcement

**Theme:** "We're live."

**Morning (6-8 AM):**
- Publish Product Hunt listing (schedule for 12:01 AM PT for max runway)
- Post launch announcement on X: origin story + link
- Post launch announcement on LinkedIn: professional version + link
- Send "We're live" email to waitlist (everyone who signed up via lead magnet or landing page)
- DM 10 pre-committed supporters to upvote on Product Hunt

**Midday (11 AM - 1 PM):**
- Share in 5 Facebook groups (Roofing Contractors Network, Storm Chasers, etc.) — value-first post, not a pitch
- Reply to every Product Hunt comment within 15 minutes
- Post an X thread: "I'm 17 and just launched an AI company. Here's everything I learned building it."

**Evening (5-7 PM):**
- Follow up with anyone who engaged on social — DM with a personal note
- Post Day 1 metrics on X (signups, conversations, first reactions)
- Send personal texts to 5 contractor contacts: "Just launched — would love your honest feedback"

**Targets:** 50 Product Hunt upvotes, 200 landing page visits, 20 email signups, 3 demo requests

## Day 2 (Tuesday): The Problem Post

**Theme:** "Here's what this solves."

**Morning:**
- Publish blog post: "How Roofing Contractors Lose $47,000/Year to Voicemail"
- Share blog post on X, LinkedIn, and in Facebook groups
- Post the "Numbers Post" from build-in-public content on X and LinkedIn

**Midday:**
- Cold DM 20 roofing contractors on LinkedIn using templates
- Reply to all Product Hunt comments from overnight
- Engage with 10 posts in roofing Facebook groups (comment with genuine advice)

**Evening:**
- Post a "Day 2 update" on X with honest metrics

- Follow up with all Day 1 demo requests — book calls for Day 3/4

**Targets:** 100 additional landing page visits, 10 new email signups, 5 LinkedIn conversations started, 2 demos booked

## Day 3 (Wednesday): The Demo Day

**Theme:** "See it in action."

**Morning:**
- Post demo video on X and LinkedIn: "Watch Alex book a job in 3 minutes"
- Share demo video in Facebook groups
- Send demo video to all email subscribers who haven't booked a call

**Midday:**
- Run 2-3 live demo calls with booked prospects
- Post the "Technical Deep Dive" build-in-public content on X
- Cold DM 20 more contractors on LinkedIn

**Evening:**
- Post "Day 3 update" on X — share what happened in the demo calls (anonymized)
- Follow up with every demo attendee within 1 hour of the call ending
- Send personalized recap emails to demo attendees with pricing and next steps

**Targets:** 3 demo calls completed, 1 closed deal, 500 demo video views

---

## Day 4 (Thursday): The Social Proof Push

**Theme:** "It's working."

**Morning:**
- If any client has signed: post the win on X and LinkedIn (with permission)
- If no client yet: post the best lead conversation screenshot (anonymized) showing Alex in action
- Share the customer story build-in-public post

**Midday:**
- Cold DM 20 more contractors on LinkedIn
- Post in Facebook groups: "Question for roofing contractors — how do you handle missed calls?" (engagement post, not a pitch)
- Follow up with all open conversations from Days 1-3

**Evening:**
- Send "last chance" email to waitlist about the Founder's Lifetime Deal: "47 spots left"
- Post "Day 4 update" on X
- Personally text any warm leads who haven't responded

**Targets:** 2 more demos booked, 1 more closed deal, Founder's Deal counter moving

---

## Day 5 (Friday): The Close

**Theme:** "Limited time. Real results."

**Morning:**
- Post the "Milestone Post" from build-in-public content — summarize the full week
- Send final launch week email to waitlist: "Here's what happened this week + your last chance at the Founder's Deal"
- Share a "Week 1 retrospective" thread on X

**Midday:**
- Follow up with every open lead, demo, and conversation from the week
- Make final push on Founder's Lifetime Deal: "40 spots left" (update real number)
- Post in Facebook groups: genuine thank-you + results from the week

**Evening:**
- Compile all metrics: visits, signups, demos, closes, revenue, PH ranking
- Write internal postmortem: what worked, what didn't, what to do next week
- Plan Week 2 content calendar based on what performed best

**Targets:** 2 total closed deals by end of week, $5,994+ in first MRR, 50+ email subscribers, 100+ PH upvotes

2. Post at least once on X and LinkedIn
3. Send at least 10 cold DMs on LinkedIn
4. Engage in at least 3 Facebook group posts
5. Update the Founder's Deal counter on the landing page
