## Product Name
LeadCatch AI

## Tagline (60 chars max)
AI that texts back missed calls in 60 seconds and books the job

## Description

Every year, the average roofing contractor loses $47,000 to unanswered phone calls. Not because the leads are bad — because nobody picks up. They're on a roof, in a meeting, driving to a job site. The homeowner hangs up, calls the next contractor on Google, and the job is gone.

LeadCatch fixes this with Alex — an AI sales assistant that texts back every missed call within 60 seconds, qualifies the lead through natural conversation, handles objections, and books the estimate directly on the contractor's calendar. It works 24/7, including nights, weekends, and during storms.

No software to learn. No staff to hire. No app to check. The contractor connects their lead sources in one onboarding call, and LeadCatch runs itself from that point forward.

Key features:
- 60-second missed call response via SMS
- AI-powered lead qualification (damage type, insurance vs. out-of-pocket, urgency)
- Automated appointment scheduling with Google Calendar
- 7-touch follow-up sequence over 14 days
- Storm Surge: proactive outreach to homeowners in storm-affected zip codes
- Automated Google review requests after job completion

Built for roofing contractors. Priced at $2,997/month with a 30-day money-back guarantee. Most clients see 10x ROI in month one.
