# LeadCatch AI — Kill Switch Decision Framework
# Purpose: Define success/failure metrics and decision triggers at Day 30
# Date: 2026-04-16

---

## The Rule

At Day 30, one of three things happens: **Double Down**, **Pivot**, or **Sunset**.

The decision is based on hard numbers, not feelings.

---

## Double Down (Green Light)

**Trigger: Hit 2 or more of these by Day 30:**

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Paying clients | 3+ |
| Monthly recurring revenue | $5,000+ |
| Demo-to-close rate | 25%+ |
| Client retention (no cancellations in month 1) | 100% |
| Inbound demo requests (people finding you, not just outbound) | 5+ |

**What to do:**
- Increase outbound volume by 2x (hire a part-time SDR or use an AI outreach tool)
- Invest in SEO content (1 blog post per week, competitor comparison pages)
- Launch the referral programme aggressively
- Start recording case studies with first clients
- Consider the DFY Installation upsell as a revenue booster
- Begin planning for adjacent verticals (HVAC, plumbing)

---

## Pivot (Yellow Light)

**Trigger: Hit 1 of the green-light metrics but missed the others.**

Examples:
- Got 3 clients but demo-to-close rate is under 15% (sales process problem, not product problem)
- Got 1 client but they're ecstatic and referring (product works, distribution is broken)
- Lots of demo requests but nobody closes (pricing or positioning problem)

**What to do depending on the failure point:**

### If sales process is broken (demos don't close):
- Record and review every demo call
- Identify the exact moment prospects drop off
- Test a new pricing structure (lower entry point, add a $597 Starter tier — already defined)
- Offer 3 free pilots to prove value, then convert

### If distribution is broken (nobody knows you exist):
- Double cold outreach volume
- Partner with a roofing coach or consultant for co-marketing
- Run a small paid ad experiment ($500 budget, Facebook targeting roofing contractors)
- Create a viral lead magnet (free ROI calculator, free audit)

### If positioning is broken (people don't understand what you do):
- Rewrite the landing page with clearer before/after messaging
- Create a 60-second explainer video
- Test a different hook: "Your AI sales rep" vs. "Never miss a lead" vs. "Book 2-4 extra jobs per week"
- Survey 10 contractors who didn't buy and ask why

### If the ICP is wrong (roofing contractors aren't buying):
- Test adjacent verticals: HVAC, plumbing, solar, landscaping
- Test a different company size: target larger companies ($5M+ revenue) with bigger budgets
- Test a different geography: focus on a single state and dominate it

---

## Sunset (Red Light)

**Trigger: Hit 0 of the green-light metrics by Day 30.**

Specifically:
- 0 paying clients after 30 days of active selling
- Less than $1,000 in total revenue (including one-time payments)
- Demo-to-close rate under 10% across 10+ demos
- Zero inbound interest (nobody seeking you out)
- Negative feedback from every demo ("too expensive," "don't need it," "already have something")

**What to do:**
1. Stop all outbound immediately — stop burning energy on a dead end
2. Analyze the 30-day data honestly: was this a distribution failure or a product-market fit failure?
3. If product-market fit failure: archive LeadCatch, extract the reusable components (AI conversation engine, Make.com automations), and apply them to a different vertical or problem
4. If distribution failure: give it 15 more days with a radically different approach (new ICP, new price point, new channel) before making a final call
5. Write a postmortem documenting every lesson learned
6. Redirect energy to the next venture
