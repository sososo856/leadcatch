# Purpose: Commission structure, payout terms, creative assets, tracking

## Programme Overview

The LeadCatch Affiliate Programme pays partners for referring roofing contractors who become paying clients. This is separate from the client referral programme — affiliates are external partners, not LeadCatch clients.

---

## Commission Structure

| Event | Commission |
|---|---|
| Referred client pays first month | 20% of first month's payment |
| Referred client stays for months 2-12 | 10% recurring monthly commission |
| Referred client upgrades plan | Commission recalculates at new plan price |

**Commission by plan:**

| Plan | First Month (20%) | Monthly Recurring (10%) | Annual Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter ($597/mo) | $119.40 | $59.70/mo | $776.10 |
| Growth ($2,997/mo) | $599.40 | $299.70/mo | $3,896.10 |
| Scale ($4,997/mo) | $999.40 | $499.70/mo | $6,496.10 |

**Founder's Lifetime Deal ($14,970):** One-time 15% commission = $2,245.50

---

## Payout Terms

- **Minimum payout:** $100
- **Payout schedule:** Monthly, on the 15th of each month for commissions earned in the previous month
- **Payout method:** Stripe Connect (direct deposit) or PayPal
- **Hold period:** 45 days after the referred client's first payment (protects against refund/chargeback)
- **Clawback:** If a referred client cancels within 60 days or requests a refund, the commission is reversed
- **Currency:** USD only

| Tier | Requirement | Bonus |

| Partner | 0-4 referrals | Standard rates (above) |
| Silver | 5-9 referrals | +2% on all commissions (22% first month, 12% recurring) |
| Gold | 10-24 referrals | +5% on all commissions (25% first month, 15% recurring) |
| Platinum | 25+ referrals | +5% + quarterly bonus ($500) + co-marketing opportunities |

## Tracking

**Affiliate link format:** `leadcatch.homes/a/{affiliate_id}`

**Tracking method:**
- First-click attribution with 90-day cookie window
- If a lead visits multiple affiliate links, the first affiliate gets credit
- Affiliate ID is passed through the signup flow and stored in Supabase

**Affiliate dashboard (provided to each affiliate):**
- Clicks on affiliate link
- Signups attributed
- Conversions (first payment)
- Commissions earned (pending and paid)
- Payout history
