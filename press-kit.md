## Company Descriptions

### Short (25 words)
LeadCatch AI is an automated lead conversion system that responds to missed calls in 60 seconds, qualifies leads, and books appointments for roofing contractors.

### Medium (75 words)
LeadCatch AI helps roofing contractors stop losing jobs to voicemail. When a call goes unanswered, our AI assistant Alex texts the caller back within 60 seconds, qualifies them through natural conversation, and books an estimate directly on the contractor's calendar. The system works 24/7 with zero effort from the contractor — no software to manage, no staff to hire. Most clients see a 25-40% improvement in lead-to-appointment rate within 30 days.

### Long (200 words)
The average roofing contractor loses $47,000 per year to unanswered phone calls. Not because they don't want the work — because they're on a roof, in a truck, or meeting a client when the phone rings. By the time they check voicemail, the homeowner has already called the next company on Google.

LeadCatch AI eliminates this problem with Alex, an AI sales assistant that responds to every missed call within 60 seconds via text message. Alex qualifies the lead through natural conversation — asking about damage type, insurance status, urgency, and location — then books the estimate directly on the contractor's Google Calendar. If the lead doesn't respond immediately, Alex follows up 7 times over 14 days.

The system is fully managed. Contractors connect their lead sources in one onboarding call and never touch the system again. Alex works 24/7, handles nights and weekends, and includes Storm Surge — proactive outreach to homeowners in areas hit by severe weather.

Founded in 2026 by 17-year-old entrepreneur Dan in Franklin, Tennessee, LeadCatch AI is built on the principle that contractors should be closing jobs, not chasing leads. The company currently serves roofing contractors across the storm belt states and plans to expand to adjacent trades.

## Founder Bio

**Dan** is a 17-year-old entrepreneur based in Franklin, Tennessee. He founded LeadCatch AI after discovering that roofing contractors lose an average of $47,000 per year to unanswered phone calls — not because they don't care about leads, but because they're physically unable to answer while doing their job.

Dan built LeadCatch using a modern automation stack (Twilio, Claude API, Make.com, Vercel) and priced it as a premium service ($2,997/month) because the ROI is undeniable — one extra booked job per week pays for the entire system several times over.

Outside of LeadCatch, Dan builds automation systems, trades futures, and believes that the best software is the kind you never have to open.
